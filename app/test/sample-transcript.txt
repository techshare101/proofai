On May 28, 2025, at approximately 10:32 AM, a verbal altercation occurred between Nurse Thompson and Patient #234 in Room 305 of St. Mary's Hospital. The incident began when Nurse Thompson entered the room to administer scheduled medication. The patient became agitated, claiming the medication was incorrect and raising their voice. Nurse Thompson maintained a professional demeanor but firmly stated the medication was correct according to the chart. The exchange lasted approximately 3 minutes, during which other staff members could hear raised voices from the hallway. Witness statements from Dr. Roberts and Nurse Chen, who were in the adjacent room, confirm hearing the altercation. The patient eventually accepted the medication after the charge nurse verified its accuracy.
